Samsung Galaxy S20 Fe
The "first" One of It's Kind
Tell Me More

The Samsung Galaxy S20 Fe stands out as a pioneer in delivering exceptional value at a budget-friendly price. While it may not boast the most extravagant features, it effectively navigates the fiercely competitive smartphone landscape by striking a balance between affordability and functionality. Earning the title of "budget king," the S20 Fe offers remarkable value for every dollar spent, ensuring optimal performance without breaking the bank.

Performance

Speed & Reliablility

Speed

The Galaxy S20 FE is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 processor, which is one of the most powerful mobile processors available. The phone is also equipped with 6GB to 8GB of RAM, which ensures that it can handle even the most demanding tasks smoothly. In our testing, the Galaxy S20 FE was able to launch apps quickly and switch between them without any lag. It was also able to handle demanding games like Fortnite and PUBG Mobile without any problems.

Reliability

Galaxy S20 FE has a reputation for being a reliable smartphone. It has not been plagued by any major software issues, and it is built with high-quality materials. In our testing, the Galaxy S20 FE was able to withstand daily wear and tear without any problems. It did not suffer from any major hardware issues, and the battery life was consistently good.

Overall

The Galaxy S20 FE is a fast and reliable smartphone. It is an excellent option for people who want a high-performance phone that they can count on.

Taking photos with the Galaxy S20 FE has been a delightful experience. It's versatile and takes stunning pictures in most lighting conditions, especially well-lit environments. The low-light performance isn't perfect, but it's still decent for a phone in this price range. Overall, the Galaxy S20 FE is a great value for photography enthusiasts who want a capable camera without breaking the bank.

Photo Editing:

The Galaxy S20 FE has several built-in photo editing apps, such as Samsung Gallery and Adobe Lightroom. These apps allow you to crop, rotate, and adjust the brightness, contrast, and saturation of your photos.

Video Editing:

The Galaxy S20 FE also has several built-in video editing apps, such as Samsung Video Editor and Adobe Premiere Rush. These apps allow you to trim, clip, and add transitions to your videos.

Web Surfing:

The Galaxy S20 FE has a large Super AMOLED display that is perfect for web surfing. The display is bright, clear, and vibrant, and it has a high refresh rate that makes scrolling through web pages smooth and responsive.

Movies:

The Galaxy S20 FE's large display and powerful speakers make it a great device for watching movies. The display is bright and clear, and the speakers are loud and clear.

Casual Gaming:

The Galaxy S20 FE can handle most casual games without any problems. It has a powerful processor and a smooth display, and the controls are responsive.

Social Media:

The Galaxy S20 FE has all of the social media apps that you need, such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. The apps are all optimized for the Galaxy S20 FE, so they run smoothly and quickly.

Some Limitations and Drawbacks

  • Overheating: The Galaxy S20 FE can sometimes get a bit hot when gaming or video editing. This is because these tasks are very demanding and require a lot of processing power. However, the phone has a built-in cooling system that helps to keep it cool, and it should not overheat to the point of being dangerous.
  • Software bugs: The Galaxy S20 FE's software is still under development, and there may be some bugs that can cause the phone to crash or malfunction. However, Samsung is regularly releasing software updates that fix these bugs, so you should experience fewer problems over time.
  • Slow fingerprint sensor: The Galaxy S20 FE's in-display fingerprint sensor is a bit slower than some other fingerprint sensors on the market. This means that it may take a little longer to unlock your phone using your fingerprint. However, the sensor is still accurate and reliable, and it should not be a major problem.
  • Nighttime photography: The Galaxy S20 FE's camera system does not perform as well in low light as some other flagship smartphones. This means that your photos may not be as clear or sharp in low-light conditions. However, the camera system is still capable of taking high-quality photos in a variety of lighting conditions.
